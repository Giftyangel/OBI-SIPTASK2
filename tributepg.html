Tribute to MS Dhoni

Former Captain of Indian Cricket Team

Mahendra Singh Dhoni,

commonly known as MS Dhoni, is a former Indian cricketer and captain of the Indian national team. He is regarded as one of the greatest captains in the history of Indian cricket.

Dhoni made his debut in December 2004 and played his last international match in July 2019. In his illustrious career, he led India to several major victories, including the 2007 ICC World Twenty20, the 2010 and 2016 Asia Cups, the 2011 ICC Cricket World Cup, and the 2013 ICC Champions Trophy.

Off the field, Dhoni is known for his calm and composed demeanor, earning him the nickname "Captain Cool". He is also a Lieutenant Colonel in the Indian Army and has received numerous awards and honors, including the Padma Shri and the Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna.

Dhoni retired from international cricket on August 15, 2020, leaving behind a legacy as one of the greatest cricketers and leaders India has ever produced.
